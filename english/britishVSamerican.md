# British vs American English

## British vs American Spelling

- pyjamas (uk) vs pajamas (us)
  - alternative for pajamas: PJs, jammies

## Vocabulary


| British   | American    |
|-----------| ------------|
| Parcel    | Package     |
| Indicator | Blinker     |
| Car park  | Parking lot |

- Indicator <-> Blinker
